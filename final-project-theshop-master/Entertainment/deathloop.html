Deathloop

$60.52

What is it?

A time-looping murder mystery where Colt has just one day to kill eight Visionaries and shut down a party at the end of the world.

Why should you play it?
Deathloop is a genuine accomplishment. With Deathloop, developer Arkane unveiled its most ambitious project to yet. Arkane is known for creating painstakingly detailed, endlessly immersive landscapes in games like Prey and Dishonored. Deathloop is a complex murder mystery in which you have one day and eight targets to kill. Time is your most essential tool. It's a game that emphasises incremental growth and observation, where your ability to piece together information is just as crucial as your proficiency with any of the game's various weapons. Naturally, it also helps that shooting is a really enjoyable haptic experience that never fails to amuse and impress. A new generation's first year was marked by Deathloop is a complex and systematic game that takes great pleasure in keeping you guessing right up until the very end. It is both the PS5's outstanding showcase and a startling window into the genre's future. Truth be told, nothing else even came close to Deathloop when the votes were totaled as the greatest game of 2021 on GamesRadar+.

Developer: Arkane Studios
Format: PC, PS5
